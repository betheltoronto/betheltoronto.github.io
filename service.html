Service

When: 12:00 PM (temporary)
Where: Downstairs in the cafeteria (temporary); Check out our address below

Due to COVID-19 we advise newcomers to join us online. For everyone's safety, please read the worship guidelines below:

  • For the safety of the congregation and community, we are complying with the municipal and provincial COVID-19 guidelines.
  • We will NOT be worshipping in the chapel like we normally do, but we will be worshipping in the cafe outside the chapel. Please note there is a maximum occupancy limit of 10.
  • Seats will be arranged in a way where social distancing protocols are observed (2m apart).
  • Worshipers are required to have masks and gloves on at all times. If you do not have any, please reach out to the leadership team.
  • There will be no fellowship or lunch served after worship, and everyone is encouraged to head straight home.
  • Please do NOT enter through the main doors. The EM congregation will be entering through the side doors from the parking lot.
  • The washrooms, kitchen, and classrooms will be closed off. Please plan your day accordingly.
  • Weekly meetings outside of Sunday worship service will continue online. We are avoiding excessive exposure and risks so we will limit meeting each other in person.
  • The church will be disinfected on Saturday, 24 hours prior to service times, and that we expect the members of the church to swiftly wipe down their seats and head out as soon as possible.
  • Wipes and hand sanitizers will be provided.

We also have a provision for giving tithes/offering:

  1. E-transfer to: torontobethel.finance@gmail.com and set security answer to "bethel" (without quotations). Add your offering number and category of offering in the memo field. For mobile users please do not add Korean characters.
  2. Mail a cheque to the church. The church address is 1155 College St. Toronto, ON M6H 1B7.
  3. Set aside and accumulate offerings each week and bring the lump sum the next time you are physically able to attend service.

Should you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to the leadership team.
