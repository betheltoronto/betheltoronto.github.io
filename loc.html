Location

Our Address

1155 College St
Toronto, ON M6H 1B7

By Car:

  • You can find us a block west of Dufferin and College.
  • Depending on where you live, you will likely be accessing our church via Allen off the 401 or the Gardiner.
  • Our parking lot has extremely limited capacity; and thus:
  • We recommend finding street parking along College or on the side streets in the immediate area. Street parking is free on Sundays including along College St.

By TTC:

From Dufferin Station on line 2 (Bloor-Danforth Subway line) - recommended

  1. Take the 29 Dufferin bus heading south
  2. Get off at College St and walk roughly a minute west along College St
  3. At Sheridan Ave you will find our church on the south side of College St
  4. Alternatively, it is a roughly 15-20 minute walk from Dufferin Station

From College or Queen's Park Station: on line 1 (Yonge-University-Spadina line)

  1. Take the 506 Carlton streetcar heading west
  2. Get off at Dufferin St and walk roughly a minute west along College St
  3. At Sheridan Ave you will find our church on the south side of College St

Notable Landmarks

  • We are a five minute walk south from Dufferin Mall.
  • Across the street from us is a large enclosed play area for the adjacent Catholic School.
  • A short one minute walk away east on College St is the lovely Brockton Haunt, a cute indepedent coffee shop and favourite amongst many members of the congregation!
