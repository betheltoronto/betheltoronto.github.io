Leadership Team

Bethel EM Leadership

The Bethel English Ministry (EM) is led by a dedicated leadership team and deacons who serve the church in capacities that God has called them to do. The leadership team (Bethel Leadership Team, or BLT for short) is comprised of members who serve specific ministries within the EM and are in charge of planning, organizing, and running their respective ministries. Deacons are officially recognized members of the church who are called to serve according to their gifts.
