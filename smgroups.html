Small Groups

We are finalizing details on the logistics and execution of a small group ministry at Bethel. We have outlined the purpose and vision of small groups as the following:

  • To create an intimate environment of trust and safety where members of the church can share and belong.
  • Small groups meet regularly for Bible study, fellowship, prayer support, and accountability. The small groups will act as the membersâ€™ core support system in times of crises.
  • The function of small groups is to integrate new believers into a vivid community, encouraging all its members to engage in discipleship and growing in faith.
  • Small groups get together to form a large congregation to participate in: Sunday morning worship services, retreats, mission trips, fellowship nights, and extra-Sunday worship events.
  • The small groups assist the church in mobilizing its members in ministry.
